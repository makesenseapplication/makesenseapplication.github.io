Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 07, 2025

SpendWise ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SpendWise mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., when configuring a private API URL). All financial data, expenses, and budget plans are stored locally on your device or on a private server controlled by you.

2. Financial Data

SpendWise is a budgeting tool. The expense data you enter is stored securely on your device. If you enable "Online Sync," your data is transmitted to the API endpoint you provide. We do not have access to the data stored on your private API.

3. Data Safety

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with third parties. Your data remains your property.

4. Service Providers

We may use third-party libraries (like Google Fonts or Firebase) to improve the application. These services may collect information used to identify you according to their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
