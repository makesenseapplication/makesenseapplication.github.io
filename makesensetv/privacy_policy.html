Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the MakesenseTV application.

1. Information Collection and Use

While using our Application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you.

A. Account Information

If you choose to use Sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture provided by Google. This information is used solely to provide you with a personalized experience and to identify your status in our Vanguard program.

B. Usage Data

When you use the Application, we may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device unique ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application.

C. Permissions

The application requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning QR codes containing playlist source data.
  • Storage/Photos: Used for uploading playlist files (.m3u, .json, .txt) and scanning QR codes from saved images.
  • Internet: Required to stream video content and fetch Electronic Program Guide (EPG) data.

2. Third-Party Services

Our application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Required for application distribution and updates.
  • AdMob: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads. Registered Vanguard testers enjoy an ad-free experience.
  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication and Vanguard status recognition.

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Any playlist sources you export are encrypted using industry-standard AES encryption. We do not store your private playlist content on our servers; it remains local to your device or is fetched directly from your provided URLs.

4. Content Disclaimer

MakesenseTV is a media player. We do not provide, host, or broadcast any media content. Users are responsible for providing their own legal content and playlists.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the support channels provided in the app listing.
